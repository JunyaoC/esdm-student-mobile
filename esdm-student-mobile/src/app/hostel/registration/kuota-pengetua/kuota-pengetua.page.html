What is Kuota Pengetua?

Kuota Pengetua is a way to book a room for your coming year in UTM. It is also opened for those who interest to have their pre-booking in the hostel they wish to stay.

How to register Kuota Pengetua?

First select the hostel you want from the hostel list. Then fill in your details according to the form. Click submit once you have done.
